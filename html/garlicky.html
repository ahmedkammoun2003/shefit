Garlic Kale Sauté With Olive Oil and Lemon Pepper

Healthy recepies

Meal Type: Side Dish
Diet Type: Vegetarian
Preparation Time: 2 minutes
Cooking Time: 5 minutes
Servings : 1
Membership: Plus

The Dish

This simple recipe is a quick and easy way to complement your main dishes with a healthy vegetable side. Kale is sautéed in a little olive oil with plenty of garlic. The lemon pepper seasoning provides a nice balance of tangy citrus notes and cracked black pepper. Any dinner would benefit from this addition, whether it's a creamy pasta dish, grilled meats, stir-fries, or hearty roasts.

Ingredients

1 tbsp
olive oil 2 cups
chopped kale 1 tbsp
garlic
peeled and minced ¼ tsp
lemon pepper seasoning

Preparation Instructions

Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, add the kale and sauté for about 2 minutes or until slightly wilted.

radio_button_uncheckedAdd the garlic and lemon pepper seasoning to the skillet, mix with the kale, and continue to sauté for another 2-3 minutes or until the kale is tender.

radio_button_uncheckedRemove from the skillet and season to taste with salt and pepper before serving.
